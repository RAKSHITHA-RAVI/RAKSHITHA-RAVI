## 🧠 What I Build

I care about one thing: turning messy, siloed data into something a business can actually act on.

That might mean engineering an ETL pipeline in Airflow, architecting a Medallion Lakehouse on Databricks, running a statistically rigorous A/B experiment across 50,000 users, building a Power BI dashboard that moves budget decisions, training a churn prediction model, or wiring up a multi-agent AI system that automates an entire analytics workflow end-to-end. The tool depends on the problem — I've worked across the full stack to solve it.

On the **engineering side**: scalable ETL/ELT pipelines, cloud data warehouses, Medallion architecture, Airflow orchestration, dbt transformations, and real-time streaming on AWS and Databricks.

On the **analytics side**: A/B experimentation frameworks, cohort retention modeling, statistical inference, executive dashboards in Power BI and Tableau, and KPI systems that drive actual decisions — not just reports.

On the **AI side**: multi-agent systems with LangGraph and Claude API, RAG pipelines with vector search and semantic retrieval, LLM evaluation frameworks, and agentic automation workflows that replace hours of manual work with intelligent, self-running processes.

From raw data to boardroom decisions — one person, the whole pipeline.
